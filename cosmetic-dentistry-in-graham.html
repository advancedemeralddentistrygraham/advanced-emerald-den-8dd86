How long does it take to get veneers or other cosmetic dental work done at your Graham office?
The timeline for cosmetic procedures depends on the specific service. For porcelain veneers, the process usually takes two to three visits over two to three weeks. The first visit involves a consultation and tooth preparation, followed by impressions. We then fit temporary veneers while your custom veneers are fabricated, and finally place the permanent veneers once they’re ready. Teeth whitening can often be completed in a single visit lasting about 90 minutes. We’re happy to work with your schedule to ensure minimal disruption.
