How long does it take to get a custom sleep apnea appliance made and fitted?
From your initial consultation at our Graham office, the process to receive your custom sleep apnea appliance usually takes about 3–4 weeks. Most patients are able to start using their appliance comfortably within a month of their first appointment.
Is Advanced Emerald Dentistry licensed to provide sleep apnea appliances in Washington state?
Yes, our dentists at Advanced Emerald Dentistry in Graham are fully licensed by the state of Washington and have specialized training in dental sleep medicine. What is the process for getting started with a sleep apnea appliance at your Graham office?
To get started, you’ll first schedule a consultation at our Graham location. During the visit, we’ll review your medical history, discuss your sleep issues, and if you have a sleep study diagnosis, we’ll use that to determine your eligibility. Once your custom device arrives (in about 2–3 weeks), you’ll come in for a fitting and adjustment, followed by periodic check-ins to ensure the appliance is working well and remains comfortable.
